About

Hey, I'm Amin 👋

I’ve spent the last decade obsessed with one question: How do you build software that scales—not just for the traffic, but for the humans maintaining it at 2 AM?

As a Lead Backend Architect, I specialize in high-stakes environments like Fintech, HealthTech, and Cryptocurrency. I’ve designed high-frequency trading engines, FHIR-compliant medical platforms, and ERP/MES systems running factory floors in real-time.

But for me, the architecture is only half the story.

🏗️ Architecture & Philosophy

I'm a firm believer that clean architecture is less about elegance and more about being kind to your future self. My core expertise lies in Node.js & TypeScript, architecting distributed systems with event-driven pipelines (RabbitMQ), real-time sync (WebSockets), and high-performance databases (PostgreSQL, ArangoDB, Redis).

🤝 Leadership & Growth

I’ve had the privilege of mentoring 30+ developers across 5 companies. I prefer spending an hour explaining the why behind a design decision over shipping a solution nobody understands. I believe a system is only as sustainable as the team behind it.

📍 Available & Ready

Currently based in Antalya, Turkey (with a transferable work permit). I support EU and North American time zones and I'm open to Lead roles or Senior Consulting—remote or on-site.
